Casey Davis

Casey Davis is a young artist born, raised, and still living in south Florida. Currently, he is working on a BFA from New World School of the Arts. He is a part of the LGBTQ+ community and strives to bring people together through their shared love of art and animals. He primarily works in illustration with a heavy interest in children's book illustration. The majority of his illustrations feature a variety of animals in greenery and floral environments.
