Kitty Does Not Belong There.

Year: 2025 | Role: Writer & Illustrator

A 6 paged story zine about an orange cat sitting in places they don't belong. My goal was to create a cohesive series of illustrations that could be both lighthearted and tell a story both verbally and environmentally. I had physical copies of the zine printed, also.
